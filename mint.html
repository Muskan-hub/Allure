HOW TO WEAR MINT GREEN COLOUR

BY TAYLA WILSON
1st April 2021
Mint Green Colour

A softer shade of turquoise and green, mint green is the perfect choice for the up and coming season. Mint fashion is everywhere,from accessories to clothing and makeup to nail polish. Flattering on almost all skin tones and a great match with peach, white, black, beige and .navy, just to name a few, what’s not to love?

Colours that Match with Mint Green

Mint green is a versatile shade that looks great when matched with a range of other colours. Pair your minty garment with white
or black pieces for a look that’s subtle yet chic. For something a little bolder, pastel colours like peach, baby blue or yellow make a perfect match. There are a variety of options to choose from, so don’t be afraid to mix and match the pieces you already own!

GET THE LOOK

Mint Shirt

Looking to change up your work attire? Why not try a mint green shirt or blouse! This garment will make the perfect addition to your office outfit. Pair it with a tailored black pencil skirt, black pumps and simple jewellery for an understated yet chic look. Want to take your minty shirt to happy hour and beyond? Simply swap your pencil skirt for white jeans, and add a brown clutch and gold hoops to finish your ensemble.

Mint Jacket or Coat

When it comes to dressing for the colder months, there’s nothing better than a warm, winter coat to keep you cozy! One in a statement colour like mint green is sure to stand out amongst the usual sea of wintery black and grey. For a head-turning winter ensemble, we recommend pairing your mint coat with smart trousers and knee-high boots. On the other hand, layering a three-quarter length coat over a skirt.
